FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What does Aashraya Foundation do?

Aashraya Foundation is a crowdfunding platform for adoptive families. We help families raise funds for their adoption by telling their story and promoting their journey through social media.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes, Aashraya Foundation is registered and approved with the IRS as a 501(c)3. (EIN: 81-0961676)

How does this work?

You make a tax-deductible donation to Aashraya Foundation to benefit one of the featured families. That family then submits their adoption expenses to Aashraya Foundation and receives grants (that your donation makes possible) to offset their adoption expenses.

Can I write a cheque to Aashraya Foundation?

Absolutely! Your donation is still tax-deductible, and can be designated for a specific family. Simply make your cheque out to "Aashraya Foundation" and remember to specify what family you'd like to donate to. Mail your cheque to:
